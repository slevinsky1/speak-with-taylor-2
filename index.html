Why This Matters

The Campus Mental Health Challenge Is Growing

Mental health challenges are now a leading contributor to stop-outs, leaves of absence, and student attrition. Without early awareness and intervention, concerns compound quietly — until they become crises that affect students, staff, and institutional outcomes.

Academic Impact

Lower GPAs, course withdrawals, and reduced completion rates follow unaddressed mental health concerns.

Increased Severity

Concerns escalate without early coping tools or intervention — small struggles become serious situations.

Limited Capacity

Long waitlists, no 24/7 support, and most students lacking tools to assess their own well-being.

"Students who are resilient and feel supported, understood, and capable of navigating challenges are significantly more likely to persist, succeed, and graduate."
